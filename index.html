That's how it Should be!

hiSbe runs on a social enterprise model that puts happiness before profits. Instead of maximising short-term profit, like big supermarkets, we pay and charge what’s fair. Employees get above the living wage, suppliers get a good deal and prices are set so customers pay a fair price for well-sourced goods. hiSbe sells everyday food, but not the big brands. We prioritise local products and work with suppliers and wholesalers who care about health, sustainability and trading fairly.

Fruit & veg

Most supermarkets import huge volumes of fruit and veg all year round. We prioritise local, seasonal and British from producers who work with nature, not against it.

The hiSbe Way

  • Saving on unnecessary food miles, resources and use of chemicals.
  • Supporting small local and British producers.
  • Making the most of what grows in its natural season.

For you this means…

A variety of fresh and naturally tasty produce that’s sourced with people and planet in mind.

Dairy

British dairy farmers are regularly forced to accept unfair prices for their milk. We support responsible brands and small dairy farmers and give them a fair price.

The hiSbe Way

  • Enabling animals to be raised in good living conditions.
  • Ensuring farmers are not forced to over-milk the animals.
  • Helping British dairy farmers stay in business.

For you this means…

Nutritious dairy products from healthy animals and happier farmers.

Meat

Most of the meat sold in supermarkets comes from factory farms here or abroad. Our meat comes directly from small, responsible family farms in Sussex.

The hiSbe Way

  • Making sure animals are reared in conditions where they can live naturally.
  • Avoiding growth hormones and unnecessary drugs.
  • Refusing meat that is dyed or bulked out with water, additives or bits of other animals.

For you this means…

Fresh high-quality meat that looks and tastes how it should.

Bread

Our bread is handmade by local bakers in Brighton and Hove and gets delivered to the store fresh every day. We offer gluten free, spelt, sourdough and rye bread, as well as white and wholemeal sliced and whole.

Fish

Many supermarkets sell species from overfished stocks and industrial scale fish farms. We only buy fish that are not on the MSC “fish to avoid list” and are completely traceable.

The hiSbe Way

  • Protecting the health of our oceans and the future supply of fish.
  • Choosing healthy, natural fresh fish that hasn’t been dyed or fed unnecessary chemicals.
  • Reducing the harmful impact of industrial fishing on the marine environment.

For you this means…

A selection of natural fresh fish that supports healthy seas.

Groceries

Big supermarkets don’t mind selling unhealthy or unethical food, as long as it makes money. We choose products from suppliers who show respect for people‘s health and welfare.

The hiSbe Way

  • Giving exposure to smaller, more responsible brands.
  • Using wholesalers recognised for their ethical policies or run as worker co-operatives.
  • Prioritising products made with real ingredients.

For you this means…

A select range of everyday groceries made up of more healthy and ethical options.

Dispensers

Supermarkets waste thousands of tons of food and packaging every day. We offer a range of good quality dry food by weight to avoid waste.

The hiSbe Way

  • Reducing the amount of food that is thrown away.
  • Preventing packaging from going to landfill.
  • Encouraging people to re-use containers they already have.

For you this means…

A way to buy as little as you need or as much as you want – and save money.

Free-from

We sell many gluten-free, wheat free and dairy free products for people who have food intolerances and, increasingly, for customers who don’t have the allergy issues, but just want to try some of these alternatives because they taste so good!

Drinks

You’ll find only the more healthy and ethical juices and fizzy drinks brands in our store. Our beers are also responsibly sourced, mostly from local and UK breweries. For our wines we prioritise organic, fairtrade and sulphur-free brands.

Household

We stock cleaning and toiletry brands that are the best in their field for being planet-friendly and responsible. We sell all your household staples in bulk: just bring your empty bottles back to the shop and refill them straight out of our taps!

Frozen

We carry healthier brands of frozen food than you would normally find in the big supermarkets, with products for both meat eaters and vegetarians, including ready meals, fruit and veg, pies, pastry, burgers, sausages, meat and fish.

Go local

For a happy community

Food that’s farmed or produced in your area is freshest and hasn’t had to travel so far.

Where possible and where the quality exists, we choose to source from producers/growers based locally.

Looking to Sussex-based suppliers first helps us provide a boost to the local economy and reduces unnecessary food miles.

It also means working more directly so we know exactly where and how the food has been produced.

Pick seasonal

For happy growing

Making the most of fruit and vegetables that are in season here means relying less on flying things in from abroad.

Growing foods out of their natural season often creates extra cost and is energy intensive.

We prioritise British produce that’s grown in season so we can bring you nature’s best while still offering great variety from season to season.

Protect nature

For a happy planet

Food is naturally more nutritious and healthy when it’s made using soil friendly farming methods that protect and help the earth rather than harming it.

We promote farming practices that protect nature, are GM-free and avoid unnecessary chemicals.

Support ethical

For happy farmers

Farmers and producers here and abroad need to get a fair deal and make a dignified living.

We consider the people behind the products when sourcing, we work directly with farmers and producers where possible and support fairer supply chains and fairly traded products.

Think welfare

For happy animals

Intensive factory farming produces cheap meat and dairy products with big knock on costs to the environment, animal welfare and our own health.

We source from local farms that prioritise welfare, don’t feed their animals growth hormones and unecessary antibiotics and don’t put their products through extra processing to bulk them out or alter appearance.

Save fish

For happy seas

Certain fish stocks have been so intensively fished that they are becoming extinct, other types are discarded (thrown back in the sea dead) in their millions.

We ensure that our fish is sourced from sustainably managed stocks, is caught in a way that causes minimal damage to the marine environment and reduces the impact of discards.

Consider waste

For a happy environment

In the UK we throw away a third of the food we buy and so much packaging that we’re running out of landfill sites.

We reduce packaging and use compostable alternatives, and help you buy food in smaller quantities.

We also don’t throw away any food that can be eaten.

Choose real

For happy bodies

It’s healthier and better value to buy fresh food instead of mass produced products with ingredients lists that look like a chemistry lesson, and that take loads of energy to produce.

We sell proper food with healthy ingredients that avoid unnecessary or harmful additives.
